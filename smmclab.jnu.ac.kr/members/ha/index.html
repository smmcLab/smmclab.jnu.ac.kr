Self-Introduction

I were a member at Smart Mobile and Media Computing Laboratory, School of Electronics and Computer Engineering, Chonnam National University, South Korea. I received B.S degree from the School of Electronics and Telecommunications, Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), VietNam in 2011. I was a solution engineer at Asian Communication Solution, JSC in 2012. I am working as a solution engineer for Electronics Communications Technology Investment Development Joint Stock Company (ELCOM) from 2015.

Research Interests

My major interests are in the research areas of Mobile Cloud Computing, Next Generation of Mobile Platform, Mobile Operating System, Peer-toPeer Network.
