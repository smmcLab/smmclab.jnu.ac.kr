Self-Introduction

I am currently a Ph.D candidate at the Smart Mobile and Media Computing Laboratory, School of Electronics and Computer Engineering, Chonnam National University, South Korea. I received my B.S degree from the School of Applied Mathematics and Informatics (SAMI), Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), Vietnam in 2013. I was an engineer at the Samsung Vietnam Mobile R&D Center in 2013. After two years, I left Samsung and pursued Master degree at SMMC laboratory from Fall semester 2015. Subsequent to my Master graduation, I decided to proceed to Ph.D degree in the same laboratory.

Research Interests

My major interests are in the research areas of Mobile Cloud Computing and Applied Mathematics in Network Communication.
