Self-Introduction

I received my B.S. Degree in Computer and Information from the Korea Nazarene University. I worked as an engineer in the System Development Team of the Media Flow Company from 2010 to 2012. I am now studying my Doctoral Degree in Electronics and Computer Engineering at Chonnam National University.

Research Interests

My research interests are in Interactive Media, Embedded systems, Digital Media and Cloud computing.
